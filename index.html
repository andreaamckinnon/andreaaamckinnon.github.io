Vacations

I love travelling. Here's where I've been recently.

Puerto Rico

February 2020

Snuck this one in right before COVID-19 hit. In addition to spending some time on the beach, I also caught the opening night of the Ricky Martin tour.

France

January 2020

Rang in the New Year in Paris while also finding some time to visit Brussels, Lyon and Dijon. The mustard, wine, and beer were fantastic.

Spain

November 2020

Travelled with my father to Madrid and Granada. Had a wonderful time exploring the history and culture, eating tapas, and drinking great wine.

Vegas

November 2020

A bachelorette party provided an escuse for my fourth Vegas trip. It was great catching up with old friends who I don't get to see often enough.

Savanah

October 2019

A great flight deal had me take a last minute day trip to this southern city. Charm was in abundance in the lovely city squares and down home cooking.

Portland, ME

October 2020

The northeast in the fall is absolutely beautiful. In my first ever trip to Portland we indulged in the craft beer scene and the great seafood.

Montauk

August 2019

A quick weekend getaway with friends was exactly what exactly what summer ordered. Pool, beach, pool, rinse, repeat.

Switzerland

August 2019

The thrid annual European bike trip had us pedaling from Geneva to Zurich. Mountains, lakes, and beauty abound.
